Greece

If you have never been to Greece, you must rectify this heresy. Rugged mountains, exotic, pristine beaches with crystal-clear waters, green valleys, quiet lakes and lush forests – Greece has it all. Greece has a plethora of beaches that seduce everyone. From sandy to pebbly beaches, isolated caves and quiet bays, you will always find somewhere you can soak up some vitamin D. Speaking of sun, Greece is known for the perfect summer. But the country is an ideal destination for every season, whether you visit it during your spring break, or as a Christmas destination.

Costa Rica

Costa Rica is one of the most biodiverse countries in the world; The lush wild natural environment can be experienced all throughout the country and is easily accessible to the curious visitor. Costa Rica is a safe paradise. The country and its people have a peaceful disposition, which makes for the perfect place to enjoy an unforgettable vacation. Costa Rica offers dreamlike landscapes, kind people willing to share their culture and provides the perfect combination of relaxation, adventure, culture, fine cuisine and wildlife. Visitors find in Costa Rica an adventure paradise set in the most beautiful natural and safe environment.

Scandinavia

Are you thinking about planning a trip to Scandinavia? Get ready for a bucket-list worthy trip to the land of the Vikings, majestic Nordic landscapes, royal capitals, fjords and waterfalls. Summer in Scandinavia, the months of July and August, is a great time to visit. While summertime may be a great time to visit the Nordic countries, it is not the best time to see the northern lights. Visiting between October and March, also known as aurora borealis season, gives you the best chance of seeing this spectacular light show.

Singapore

This little country on the Malay Peninsula invites you to visit its landmarks. The country offers plenty of activities and a staggering spectacle of a play of lighting and architecture. Singapore is the country’s capital and its only city. It’s a large business and shopping centre. One of the greatest attractions includes Gardens by the Bay that consist of three parts – southern, eastern and central – and are home to more than half a million plants. The Flower Dome, the world’s largest greenhouse and The River Safari theme park just begs you to explore it.

Kenya-Tanzania

Try some border hopping in east Africa – travelling between Kenya and Tanzania delivers more wildlife, diverse landscapes and a fascinating taste of two very different countries. This interesting contrast is one reason for travelling in both countries, another is the chance to experience a variety of unforgettable locations, including the Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, Masai Mara and Lake Victoria in one trip. Happily, they form a loop encircling the border, with distances between each manageable by road.

Australia

Australia is abundant with unique experiences and awe-inspiring landscapes. There’s a well-known vibrancy in its natural beauty. Discover hidden gems while creating deep connection with Australia’s culture, community and environment. Even just minutes from a major city you can discover rainforests and red deserts, see ancient Aboriginal rock art sites, climb snow-capped mountains. From city beaches to secluded spots where you’ll share the sand with more wildlife than humans, there are gorgeous beaches and islands across the country.
